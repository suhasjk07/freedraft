Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

Data We Collect: FreeDraft collects account information (name, email), contract data you create, and usage analytics to improve the platform.

How We Use It: Your data is used solely to provide the FreeDraft service. We never sell your data to third parties, use it for advertising, or share it without your consent.

Data Security: All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We are ISO 27001 certified and SOC 2 Type II audited.

Your Rights (GDPR): You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your data at any time. Contact us at privacy@freedraft.io.

Data Retention: Account data is retained for 30 days after account deletion. Contract data can be exported before deletion.

Contact: For privacy inquiries, email privacy@freedraft.io or write to FreeDraft Inc., Bengaluru, Karnataka, India.
